How Our Mentorship Works

Our structured program ensures meaningful connections and measurable impact.

Application & Screening

Complete our online application and participate in a screening interview to ensure the best mentor-mentee matches.

Training & Orientation

Receive comprehensive training on effective mentorship techniques, cultural sensitivity, and our program framework.

Mentor-Mentee Matching

We carefully match you with a mentee based on personality, interests, and goals for optimal compatibility.

Structured Engagement

Follow our guided curriculum with monthly themes while building a personal connection through regular meetings.

Ongoing Support

Access resources, check-ins with our team, and mentor community events throughout your journey.

Mentor Requirements

We look for mentors who are committed, empathetic, and passionate about youth development.

Basic Qualifications

  • Minimum age of 21 years
  • Commitment of at least 1 year
  • Ability to meet with mentee at least twice monthly
  • Clean background check
  • Strong communication skills

Ideal Mentor Qualities

  • Empathetic and patient listener
  • Positive role model
  • Open to learning and growth
  • Cultural sensitivity
  • Passion for youth development

Mentor Terms & Conditions

Commitment

By applying to become a mentor with Apex Rise, you agree to:

  • Commit to at least one year of mentorship
  • Meet with your mentee at least twice per month
  • Participate in required training sessions
  • Submit periodic progress reports

Confidentiality

You agree to maintain strict confidentiality regarding all personal information about your mentee and their family, and to adhere to our child protection policies at all times.

Background Check

All mentors must pass a background check before being matched with a mentee. There is no cost to you for this process.

Code of Conduct

Mentors must adhere to our Code of Conduct, which includes guidelines for appropriate communication, meeting locations, and professional boundaries.

Termination

Apex Rise reserves the right to terminate the mentor relationship at any time if program guidelines are not followed or if it's determined to be in the best interest of the mentee.

Application Submitted!

Thank you for your interest in becoming an Apex Rise mentor. Our team will review your application and contact you within 5-7 business days.
