Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about getting involved with Apex Rise.

What qualifications do I need to volunteer?

We welcome volunteers from all backgrounds. While specific roles may require certain skills, most importantly we look for empathy, commitment, and a passion for helping children. We provide all necessary training for our programs.

How much time do I need to commit?

Time commitments vary by role. Program facilitators typically commit 4-8 hours per week, while mentors usually spend 2-4 hours weekly with their mentee. We also have flexible digital volunteering opportunities that can fit your schedule.

Can I volunteer remotely?

Yes! While some roles require in-person presence in our program villages, we have many digital volunteering opportunities including online mentoring, content creation, translation, and more that can be done remotely.

How are donations used?

85% of donations go directly to program expenses including materials, facilitator training, and village outreach. 10% supports organizational operations, and 5% goes to fundraising efforts. We're committed to financial transparency.

Can I visit the programs I support?

Absolutely! We encourage donors to see our work firsthand. We organize regular visits to our program villages where you can meet the children and communities benefiting from your support.
