Frequently Asked Questions

Are these workshops really free?

Yes! Thanks to our donors, all workshops are completely free for parents from rural communities.

What if I can't attend in person?

We offer online participation options for all workshops. Just indicate your preference when registering.

Can both parents attend?

Absolutely! We encourage both parents to participate when possible.

What language are the workshops in?

Workshops are conducted in Hindi and local languages based on participant needs.
