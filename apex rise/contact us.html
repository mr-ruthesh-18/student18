Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about Apex Rise and our programs.

How can I enroll my child in Apex Rise programs?

You can enroll your child by contacting our local coordinator in your village or by filling out the enrollment form on our website. We currently operate in 120+ villages across India, and our team will help determine if we have programs available in your area.

What are the eligibility criteria for your programs?

Our programs are designed for children aged 10-18 from rural communities. There are no academic or financial eligibility requirements - we believe every child deserves the opportunity to build confidence and emotional resilience.

How can I volunteer with Apex Rise?

We welcome volunteers in various capacities - as mentors, program facilitators, administrative support, and more. Please fill out our volunteer application form or contact our volunteer coordinator at volunteers@apexrise.org for more information.

Are donations to Apex Rise tax-deductible?

Yes, Apex Rise is a registered non-profit organization, and all donations are tax-deductible under Section 80G of the Income Tax Act in India. Donors outside India should consult their local tax laws.

How can I bring Apex Rise programs to my village?

We're always looking to expand our reach. Please contact our partnerships team at partnerships@apexrise.org with details about your community, and we'll explore possibilities for collaboration.
