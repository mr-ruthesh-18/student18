About This Program

This program is designed to help children develop essential digital skills that will serve them in education and future careers. Through hands-on activities and practical projects, participants gain confidence with technology and learn how to use digital tools effectively.

The program is specially adapted for rural students, starting with foundational skills and building up to more advanced applications based on each student's progress.

Program Structure

  • Weekly hands-on sessions
  • Small group format
  • Project-based learning
  • Progress assessments
  • Showcase event

Skills You'll Learn

  • Basic computer operation
  • Internet research skills
  • Email and online communication
  • Word processing and spreadsheets
  • Online safety and privacy

Who Should Attend

This program is ideal for students who:

  • Have limited experience with computers
  • Want to use technology for schoolwork
  • Are curious about digital careers
  • Need to overcome fear of technology
