Our Impact Areas

How we're making a difference in the lives of village children

Communication Skills

85% of participants show improved ability to express themselves clearly and confidently in public settings.

Academic Performance

Students in our programs demonstrate a 40% increase in classroom participation and 25% improvement in grades.

Growth Mindset

78% of children develop a stronger belief in their ability to learn and improve through effort.

Social Integration

Reduction in social anxiety reported by 89% of participants after completing our programs.

Digital Literacy

Over 2,000 children trained in essential digital skills, bridging the urban-rural technology gap.

Emotional Well-being

Significant improvements in self-reported happiness and emotional resilience metrics.

Our Journey

Milestones in our mission to empower village children

2018

Founded in Bihar

Launched our first program in 5 villages, reaching 120 children with confidence-building workshops.

2019

Expansion to Odisha

Extended our reach to 15 new villages and trained our first cohort of 30 local mentors.

2020

Digital Adaptation

Pivoted to hybrid programs during pandemic, developing online resources reaching 500+ children.

2021

Government Partnership

Collaborated with state education departments to integrate our curriculum in 50 rural schools.

2022

National Recognition

Awarded the National Education Innovation Prize for our measurable impact on rural education.

2023

5,000 Children Empowered

Celebrated reaching our 5,000th participant and expanding to 120 villages across 5 states.
