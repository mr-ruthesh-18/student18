Volunteer FAQs

Answers to common questions about volunteering with Apex Rise.

What's the time commitment for volunteers?

Time commitments vary by role. Mentors typically commit 2-3 hours per week for at least 6 months. Workshop facilitators might commit to 1-2 weekends per month. We also have project-based roles with flexible hours. During the application process, we'll discuss options that fit your availability.

Do I need prior experience working with children?

While experience is helpful, it's not required for most roles. We provide comprehensive training to all volunteers. What's most important is your passion for our mission, willingness to learn, and commitment to the children we serve.

Can I volunteer remotely?

Some roles like content development, translation, and certain mentoring positions can be done remotely. However, most workshop and community coordination roles require in-person participation in the villages we serve. We'll work with you to find the best fit based on your location and preferences.

What training will I receive?

All volunteers complete our core training program covering child development, emotional intelligence, and our confidence-building methodology. Role-specific training follows. Mentors receive additional coaching and ongoing support. Training is a mix of online modules and live sessions (virtual or in-person).

Are there any age requirements to volunteer?

You must be at least 18 years old to volunteer independently. We do have some opportunities for younger volunteers (16+) to assist with events or digital projects under supervision. Our most experienced volunteers often bring valuable life experience, so we welcome applicants of all ages.

How does the application process work?

After submitting your application, our team will review it within 5-7 business days. Qualified candidates will be invited for an interview (usually virtual). Successful applicants then complete background checks and training before being matched with their role. The entire process typically takes 2-4 weeks depending on the role.
