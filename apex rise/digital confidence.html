About the Program

Our Digital Confidence program is specifically designed to empower rural children with the technological skills and digital literacy they need to thrive in today's connected world. Many village children face a "digital inferiority complex" when interacting with their urban peers. This program eliminates that gap.

8 Weeks
Ages 10-18
Free for Villages

Who Should Join?

This program is ideal for village children who:

  • Feel intimidated by technology
  • Lack access to digital education
  • Want to pursue careers requiring tech skills
  • Need confidence in online communication

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our Digital Confidence program

Who is eligible for this program?

The Digital Confidence program is open to all village children ages 10-18. No prior computer experience is required - we start from the very basics. The program is completely free for participants from rural areas.

What equipment is needed?

All necessary equipment is provided during sessions at our learning centers. For remote participants, we can work with whatever technology is available in your community, even if it's just a shared smartphone.

How is this different from regular computer classes?

While we teach technical skills, our primary focus is building confidence and overcoming the psychological barriers that many rural children face with technology. We combine skill-building with emotional empowerment.

Can this help with school or future jobs?

Absolutely! The digital skills taught in this program are directly applicable to school projects and are increasingly required for most careers. We also specifically cover how these skills can lead to employment opportunities.

What if I miss some sessions?

We understand that village life can be unpredictable. Our mentors will work with you to catch up on missed material, and all lesson summaries are available digitally for review.
