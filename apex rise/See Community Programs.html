Program Implementation

Our phased approach ensures sustainable community engagement

Phase 1: Community Assessment

3-month deep dive understanding village dynamics, existing support systems, and cultural context.

  • Stakeholder interviews
  • Resource mapping
  • Cultural alignment

Phase 2: Capacity Building

6-12 month intensive training program for local leaders and volunteers.

  • Leadership workshops
  • Emotional intelligence training
  • Program management skills

Phase 3: Sustainable Systems

Ongoing support for community-led initiatives and impact monitoring.

  • Monthly check-ins
  • Resource library access
  • Annual impact reviews
