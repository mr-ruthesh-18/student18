Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about joining our movement

Do I need prior experience to volunteer?

No prior experience is necessary for most volunteer roles. We provide comprehensive training to all our volunteers. What's most important is your passion for helping children and willingness to learn.

What is the time commitment for volunteers?

We have opportunities to fit various schedules. Most volunteers commit 4-8 hours per week, but we also have project-based and seasonal opportunities with flexible time commitments.

Can I volunteer remotely?

Some roles like digital mentoring, content creation, and administrative support can be done remotely. However, most of our direct work with children requires in-person participation.

Is there an age requirement to volunteer?

Volunteers must be at least 18 years old. We have opportunities suitable for college students, working professionals, and retirees alike.

How are donations used?

85% of donations go directly to program costs including materials, training, and community outreach. 10% supports administrative costs, and 5% goes to fundraising efforts. We're committed to financial transparency.

Can I start an Apex Rise chapter in my area?

Yes! We're always looking to expand to new communities. After completing our volunteer training, qualified individuals can apply to start a local chapter with our support and resources.
