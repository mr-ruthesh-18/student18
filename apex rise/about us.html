Who We Are

Apex Rise is a non-profit organization dedicated to empowering village children with the confidence and emotional resilience they need to succeed in life.

Our Mission

To provide practical emotional solutions that help rural children overcome fear, build self-esteem, and develop the confidence to face society's challenges. We believe every child, regardless of their background, deserves the opportunity to realize their full potential.

Our Vision

A world where village children stand tall with confidence, free from the constraints of self-doubt and societal limitations. We envision rural communities where children grow up believing in their abilities and contributing meaningfully to society.

Our Core Values

These principles guide everything we do at Apex Rise

Empathy

We listen deeply to understand the unique challenges faced by village children and design solutions that truly meet their needs.

Empowerment

We believe in giving children the tools and confidence to shape their own futures, rather than creating dependency.

Community

We work with families, teachers, and local leaders to create sustainable change that benefits entire communities.

Innovation

We continuously develop and refine our approaches based on evidence and the changing needs of rural India.

Equity

We prioritize reaching the most marginalized children who face the greatest barriers to confidence and success.

Sustainability

We build programs that create lasting change by training local mentors and developing community ownership.

Our Journey

From humble beginnings to a movement transforming thousands of lives

2015

Founding

Apex Rise was founded by three friends who witnessed firsthand how lack of confidence limited opportunities for bright village children.

2016

First Program

Launched our pilot confidence-building workshop in 5 villages of Bihar, reaching 120 children in the first year.

2018

Expansion

Expanded to three states with a team of 15 trained mentors and a refined curriculum based on our learnings.

2020

Digital Transformation

Adapted our programs for online delivery during the pandemic, unexpectedly increasing our reach to remote areas.

2023

National Recognition

Received the National Award for Excellence in Rural Education from the Ministry of Education for our innovative approach.
