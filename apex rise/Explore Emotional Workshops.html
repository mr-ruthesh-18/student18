Workshop Components

Our comprehensive curriculum addresses all aspects of emotional development

Emotional Vocabulary

Teaching children to identify and name 40+ emotions through interactive games and role-playing

Empathy Building

Group activities that foster understanding of different perspectives and compassionate responses

Mindfulness Practice

Simple meditation and breathing techniques for emotional regulation and stress management

Conflict Resolution

Practical strategies for resolving disagreements while maintaining emotional balance

Workshop Structure

12-week program with progressive learning modules

Foundation Phase (Weeks 1-4)

Emotion identification, basic regulation techniques, and trust-building exercises

Development Phase (Weeks 5-8)

Advanced emotional management, empathy development, and group collaboration

Application Phase (Weeks 9-11)

Real-world scenarios, conflict resolution practice, and community projects

Celebration Phase (Week 12)

Graduation ceremony, family participation, and ongoing support planning
