Partnership FAQs

What is the minimum commitment for corporate partners?

We work with partners at all levels. Our smallest corporate partnerships start at ₹50,000 annually, but we can tailor programs to fit your budget and goals.

How are funds utilized?

85% of funds go directly to program implementation, 10% to monitoring and evaluation, and 5% to administrative costs. We provide detailed financial reports to all partners.

Can we specify which program to support?

Absolutely! Many partners choose to support specific programs like Confidence Builders or Future Leaders. We can also co-create customized initiatives.

What recognition do partners receive?

Partners are recognized on our website, annual report, and at events. Higher-level partners receive co-branding opportunities and media coverage.

How long does the partnership process take?

After initial contact, we typically finalize partnerships within 2-4 weeks, depending on complexity. We move quickly to start creating impact together.
